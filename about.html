About Localyze Master Catalog

Localyze Master Catalog is the internal backend system that powers the entire Localyze ecosystem. It is an admin-only portal designed to create, manage, and control every brand, product, and category across the platform.

This system allows seamless management of all catalog data, ensuring accuracy, speed, and consistency throughout the Localyze customer app.

Purpose of the Master Catalog

  • To maintain a centralized catalog of all brands and products
  • To manage both branded and non-branded items
  • To provide clean, structured data to the Localyze main application
  • To allow quick updates, edits, and expansions of inventory
  • To import large catalog files via Excel or URLs
  • To standardize product names, images, pricing, and attributes

Key Features

  • Add, edit, or remove brands
  • Manage product details including price, description, categories
  • Support dynamic fields for custom product attributes
  • Handle non-branded grocery items
  • Bulk import catalog via Excel or URL
  • Real-time syncing with the Localyze main app
  • Centralized data control for accuracy and consistency

Why We Built It

The grocery market contains thousands of products with varying units, packaging sizes, and pricing structures. Managing this manually is slow and error-prone.

Localyze Master Catalog provides:

  • Full control
  • Speed
  • Scalability
  • Data accuracy
  • Seamless integration with our customer-facing platform

It is the backbone that keeps Localyze organized, efficient, and ready to scale.
